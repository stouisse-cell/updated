International Buyers

The Market Has Answers.
You Just Need the Right Questions.

Morocco's real estate market is not short of opportunity. What it is short of is organised information. We help international buyers get the clarity they need before they commit.

Who This Is For

The Details
Make the Difference.

Morocco's real estate market is full of opportunity and short on organised information. Many good projects are still sold through contacts and direct calls, with limited material available online. The right decision depends on more than price — it depends on developer credibility, exact specifications, legal structure, and what happens after the purchase. Those are the details that determine whether an opportunity is actually a good decision.

01

Relocation & Residence

For buyers planning to live in Morocco, we focus on practical use: location, accessibility, building quality, and long-term livability — not just the purchase price.

02

Investment & Rental Use

For return-focused buyers, we look beyond the listing — tenant demand, rental setup, furnishing requirements, management structure, and resale logic.

03

Buying From Abroad

For buyers outside Morocco, we verify what is difficult to assess remotely: project details, developer credibility, legal steps, banking structure, and ongoing coordination.

How We Work With You

A Structured Process,
Start to Finish.

Whether you are visiting Morocco or buying entirely from abroad, the process begins with a structured consultation — in person, by video, or by phone. This is where we establish the foundation: why you want to buy, what the property needs to achieve, your budget, your timeline, and your expected level of ongoing involvement.

01

We Build Your Buyer Profile

We clarify the purpose of the purchase — relocation, rental income, second residence, long-term hold — and translate that into a specific acquisition framework: location type, budget range, property type, and the level of management you want after purchase.

02

Market Orientation and Strategy Alignment

Before presenting properties, we give you context: pricing by area and product type, the difference between new construction, resale, and pre-construction, developer positioning, rental realities where relevant, and the risks attached to your strategy.

03

We Shortlist and Compare Opportunities

We build a structured shortlist from our network of vetted developers and ongoing market research, comparing each option across price, location quality, developer credibility, technical specifications, and suitability for your intended use.

04

Remote Validation Before You Travel

For buyers coordinating from abroad, we remove uncertainty before any trip — first-level screenings, video walkthroughs, initial assessments, and elimination of weak options so your time in Morocco is spent evaluating rather than discovering.

05

We Inspect, Document, and Report

For each serious option, we conduct a detailed in-person evaluation with photo and video documentation, floor plan review, surface area verification where possible, and inspection of materials, finishes, fixtures, and overall build quality. We also assess neighbourhood quality, access, noise, and livability.

06

Financial, Legal, and Developer Verification

We review the developer's track record, available legal documentation, payment schedule structure, and title or registration status. We also help clarify the banking structure for non-resident buyers — convertible dirham account, repatriation guarantee, and financing options.

07

Decision Structuring and Negotiation Guidance

Once a property is selected, we confirm it still matches your original objective, identify trade-offs clearly, advise on pricing and negotiation positioning, and help structure reservation and payment timing without pushing you into a rushed commitment.

08

Reservation and Transaction Coordination

We stay involved through the transaction stage — reservation process, documentation review, coordination with the developer, payment tracking, and communication follow-up so delays and misunderstandings do not build up while you are away.

09

Post-Purchase Follow-Up

Our role does not stop at reservation. For new developments, we continue with construction follow-up, developer communication, delivery updates, rental setup coordination, and support if issues appear after you have returned abroad.

Our process is designed to reduce uncertainty at every stage — from the first consultation through post-purchase follow-up. Each step builds on the previous one so decisions are made with structure, context, and verification.
