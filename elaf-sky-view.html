Project Overview

High-Spec Apartments
in Centre Ville Kénitra.

Elaf Sky View introduces thoughtfully designed apartments in one of Morocco's fastest-growing cities, with a rare balance of affordable luxury, practical infrastructure, and everyday convenience.

Kénitra · City Centre
Elaf Sky View
Apartments Centre Ville Energy Ready High-Spec Finishes Daam Sakani Eligible Active Mandate

Located in Centre Ville, in a well-established green neighbourhood beside a public garden, Elaf Sky View places residents about 10 minutes on foot from the TGV station and 5 minutes by car from the motorway.

Designed for both rental income and long-term living, each unit includes air-conditioning pre-installation, double-insulated windows and glass doors, Turkish-imported doors with triple deadbolt systems, fully equipped kitchens, and built-in shower seating.

Each apartment also benefits from private rooftop space for future solar panels, directly connected to the unit. Underground parking is assigned to each apartment and included in the property title.
